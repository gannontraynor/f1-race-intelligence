# Engineering Principles

This project exists to demonstrate software engineering, not simply produce an application.

## Every feature should

- Solve a real problem
- Be independently testable
- Have clear ownership
- Be observable
- Fail gracefully
- Be documented

## AI Principles

- AI never performs calculations.
- AI never invents data.
- AI explains deterministic results.
- Every AI response should be reproducible from underlying analytics.

## Data Principles

- Raw data is immutable.
- Transformations are reproducible.
- Analytics are deterministic.
- Every metric should be traceable to source data.

## Product Principles

- Fast over flashy.
- Simple over clever.
- Interactive over static.
- Accurate over impressive.
